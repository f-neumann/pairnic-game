About the game

A quiet puzzle with a clever twist

Pairnic is a brain-teasing puzzle game about sliding blocks into place. Every level is a small hand-built playfield of walls and colored blocks. You slide blocks left and right — gravity does the rest, pulling them down until they land on a wall, another block, or the floor. Get two or more blocks of the same symbol touching, and they vanish. Clear every block on the board to win the level, before your timer runs out.

The signature twist: some levels hide an odd number of a given symbol — three lightning bolts, say, with no way to pair them off two at a time. The only way to clear them is to engineer a moment where all three touch at once. It's a puzzle within the puzzle, and it's the heart of what makes Pairnic click.

Slide, Drop, Match
Simple to learn: slide blocks, let gravity pull them into place, and clear matching symbols. No combos to memorize, no reflexes required.
The Odd-Count Twist
Some symbols only come in threes. Pairing two off first strands the last one forever — you have to make all three meet at once.
Moving Platforms
Later stages add platforms that carry blocks up, down, and sideways on their own schedule — timing becomes part of the solution.
Drag or Cursor Control
Drag blocks directly, or switch to tap-to-select cursor mode in Settings if you'd rather not drag on glass.
Four Block Themes
Re-skin every block as Classic icons, Fruits, Flowers, or Faces — purely cosmetic, switch anytime in Settings, even mid-level.
No Ads, No Accounts
No in-app purchases, no ad breaks, no sign-in. Your progress stays on your device and syncs privately across your own devices via iCloud.
How to play

Learn it in a minute

Objective

Clear every block on the board before the timer runs out. Slide blocks left or right; gravity pulls them down onto whatever's beneath — a wall, another block, or the floor.

Controls

👆
Drag mode
Drag a block left or right to slide it. Each cell you cross is a committed step — release your finger and the block stays put.
◎
Cursor mode
Tap a block to select it, then swipe to move it. An accessibility-friendly alternative to dragging, switchable anytime in Settings.
↺
Undo
Up to 3 undos per level. One undo rewinds your entire last move, including any chain reactions — everything except the clock.
+30
Time extension
A one-time +30 second top-up, available only once you're down to 30 seconds or less — a rescue, not a shortcut.

Star rating

★☆☆Clear the level.
★★☆Clear it without using undo.
★★★Clear it above the level's target score — bigger matches, simultaneous groups, and chain reactions all score more.
Strategy

Tips & tricks

  1. Watch for odd counts. If a symbol shows up three times on a board, pairing two of them off first strands the last one — plan a moment where all three can touch at once instead.
  2. Undo is for experiments, not just mistakes. Three undos per level fully restores the board (except the clock), so use one to try a risky idea and see what happens.
  3. The +30s button saves itself for when you need it. It's locked until the timer hits 30 seconds, so there's no reason to hold back — it can't be banked for later.
  4. Platforms move on their own schedule. Matching a block riding a platform is often about timing your slide to when it arrives, not just where it sits right now.
  5. Think a few moves ahead. You only ever slide left or right — gravity handles the falling. Plan for where a block will land, not just where you're pushing it from.
  6. Not your style? Switch it up. Cursor control and four block themes are both free to try anytime in Settings — even mid-level.
Questions

FAQ

Is Pairnic free to play? ▶
Check the App Store listing for the current price. There are no in-app purchases, no ads, and no subscriptions — you get the complete game with all 100 levels.
Does Pairnic collect my data? ▶
Only your own progress — which levels you've cleared, your stars, and your best scores. It's stored on your device and synced privately across your own devices via Apple's iCloud, using your existing iCloud account. Nothing is ever sent to the developer. There are no accounts, no analytics SDKs, and no ad frameworks in the app.
What's the "three at once" thing about? ▶
Normally you clear a symbol by getting two or more of it touching. Some levels instead hide an odd count — three of a kind — so pairing two off first leaves the last one unmatchable. You have to engineer a single moment where all three touch simultaneously.
Can I switch between drag and cursor controls? ▶
Yes — it's a toggle in Settings, and it applies immediately, even in the middle of a level.
What happens if a level gets stuck? ▶
If the board reaches a state where it can no longer be solved (a symbol left with no possible partner), Pairnic tells you and lets you retry immediately — no penalty beyond starting the level over. Every shipped level has also been verified solvable ahead of time by an exhaustive solver, so if it's in the game, it can be cleared.
Does it work on iPad? ▶
Yes, full iPhone and iPad support, including both iPad orientations.
What iOS version is required? ▶
Pairnic requires iOS 18.0 or later.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026 · Published by Frank Neumann

What we collect

Nothing that identifies you. Pairnic has no user accounts, no login, and no registration of any kind. The app includes no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, no crash reporters, and no third-party tracking code — only first-party Apple frameworks (SpriteKit, SwiftUI, AVFoundation).

Your game progress

Which levels you've cleared, your stars, and your best scores are stored locally on your device as a small file. That same data is mirrored through Apple's iCloud key-value store so it follows you across your own devices signed into the same iCloud account. This data is never transmitted to the developer or any third party — Pairnic has no backend server at all.
